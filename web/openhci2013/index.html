OpenHCI 2013

OpenHCI 2013 is the 4th annual student-organized workshop on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). The workshop aims to share preliminary knowledge of user experience (UX) design and engineering, and to exploit potential power of inter-disciplinary communication and collaboration.

Every year, more than 100 students join OpenHCI workshop. Through a series of courses, post-lesson activities and hands-on labs in the 6-day workshop, participants can experience the whole HCI design process, realize ideas with partners, and show their innovation to attract wide publicity!
